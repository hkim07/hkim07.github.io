Hyunuk Kim

Ph.D. Candidate in Industrial and Management Engineering

Pohang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH)

I am Ph.D. candidate in Industrial and Management Engineering at Pohang University of Science and Technology, and a visiting pre-doctoral fellow at the Kellogg School of Management, Northwestern University. My research interests are computational social science, network science, innovation, and technology foresight, especially focusing on how collective and evolutionary behaviors create knowledge in the past, present, and future.
